What Are We Here For?

Track is a sport which requires number of strategies. In comparision to other sports, a track athlete's progress is clear in numbers. We are here to help record times for track runners, and we give time improvement predictions with graphs for the end of the season along with workouts catered towards each individual.

How Does It Work?

You can either log in and store your times for future use, or just use the logging services without signing in. If you are using the service without an account, just put in all of the times that you have and see it get squiggly graphed in no time! We also include your time prediction for the end of the season on the graph, so you can get a sense of how close or far you are to reaching that. We provide you with training regimens depending on your time so that you can just focus on bettering your time with no need to worry about choosing an excercise out of the thousands out
Run your butts off Rhinos!

What Do We Have In Mind For The Future?

We are hoping to get our database set to store user data by the end of the year, and our developers are working hard to get this service ready for use for runners of all distances by spring of 2023. We are also always working towards a more accurate prediction system so that you never have anything to doubt. Our medical sources are expanding as you read this, and we are getting more and more information so that this can become the one-stop-shop for any and all runners, whether that be for workouts, equipment, injuries, or food. Be excited to see how we innovate the world of track:)
