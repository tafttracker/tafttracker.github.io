A Track Diary for Trackstars

In Mr. Rane's Python class, Rina, Marina, and Steven came up with the idea of creating a website for track diary/predictions for their fellow track stars at Taft.

Why do I need a track diary?

In order to run faster by 0.1 second step by step, runners need a diary to keep track of their progress.
